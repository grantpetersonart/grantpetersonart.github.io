bio

Grant Peterson is a third generation artist and a musician who spent 17 years growing up in Beaufort, SC. He now lives and works from Charleston, SC. He is an accomplished cellist of over 10 years, performing with local orchestras, ensembles, and various groups.

In 2022 he received his Bachelor's of Fine Arts from the College of Charleston for Music Performance and Studio Art. His paintings have been featured in the Halsey Institute of Contemporary art Young Contemporaries series. Updated June 2022.
